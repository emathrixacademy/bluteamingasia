Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our plans and platform.

What is included in the Free plan?
The Free plan includes up to 5 cameras, 10 devices, and 1,000 events per day. You get basic AI investigation capabilities with our 9-stage threat detection pipeline. It is ideal for small offices or individuals who want to evaluate the platform before upgrading.
How does the AI Investigation feature differ between plans?
The Free plan offers basic AI investigation with manual trigger and limited scope. Professional and Enterprise plans unlock full autonomous AI that continuously monitors events, correlates cross-domain threats, generates forensic timelines, and can autonomously execute response actions such as locking doors or isolating network segments.
What does Private Deployment mean?
Private Deployment, available on the Enterprise plan, means the entire BlueteamingAsia platform is deployed on your own infrastructure -- whether on-premise or in your private cloud. No data ever leaves your network. This option is designed for organizations with strict compliance or sovereignty requirements.
What are the Robotics & Drones capabilities?
Enterprise plans include integration with security robots and drones for autonomous patrol, perimeter monitoring, and incident response. The AI can dispatch drones to investigate alerts, provide live video feeds from hard-to-reach areas, and coordinate robotic patrols based on threat intelligence.
Can I upgrade my plan at any time?
Yes. You can upgrade from Free to Professional at any time through your account settings. Upgrading to Enterprise requires contacting our sales team for a customized deployment plan. All existing data, device configurations, and historical events are preserved during the upgrade.
Do you offer a trial for the Professional plan?
Yes. We offer a 14-day free trial of the Professional plan with full access to all features including unlimited devices, advanced analytics, and API integrations. No credit card is required to start. Contact us to request a trial.
